About The Conference

The 12th International Conference on Innovation and New Visions: Perspectives on Business and Law in the 21st Century is a premier event that brings together researchers, scholars, and industry experts to explore the latest trends, challenges, and innovations in the fields of business and law.

Hosted by the Faculty of Business and Law, Elbasan, this conference offers a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and insightful discussions on emerging topics.
