Welcome to the 12th FBL International Conference titled: “Economic Policies, Business Perspectives, and Sustainability Transformation.”

This academic event offers researchers and practitioners the opportunity to explore a wide range of topics at the intersection of economic policy, business innovation, and sustainability. We invite you to explore our comprehensive list of conference topics outlined below, highlighting the diverse and relevant areas that will drive research, dialogue, and collaboration.

Join us in shaping the future of economic and sustainable development through insightful contributions and collective expertise!
