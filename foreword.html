Foreword by the Dean of FBL

  1. Home > Foreword by the Dean of FBL

Shpëtim Çerri

Dean of Faculty of Business and Law

The Faculty of Business and Law at University of Elbasan “Aleksandër Xhuvani” is delighted to announce the upcoming 12th International Conference “Innovation and New Visions: Perspectives on Business and Law in the 21st Century”, a multidisciplinary platform designed to explore the challenges and opportunities arising from today’s dynamic and unpredictable global environment. This conference seeks to foster dialogue among academics, researchers, and practitioners, addressing the complex interplay of economics, law, and social policies in shaping our collective future.

In an era defined by rapid change and increasing uncertainty, the challenges posed by environmental degradation and climate change demand immediate and innovative responses. This conference seeks to explore how businesses, governments, and legal systems can adapt to these pressing issues. By developing green laws and policies, fostering sustainable supply chains, and implementing strategies for economic resilience, stakeholders can navigate this unpredictable environment and contribute to a more stable and sustainable future.

The shift toward a green economy represents a crucial step in achieving sustainable development. As the global economy embraces environmental sustainability, businesses and policymakers alike must consider how to integrate eco-friendly practices into operational models. The role of public-private partnerships in fostering green innovations will be a focal point of discussion, alongside the legal frameworks and mechanisms that support these transitions. Such efforts not only address ecological concerns but also open new pathways for innovation and economic growth.

Another transformative force shaping the modern world is artificial intelligence (AI). With its capacity to revolutionize industries and redefine business operations, AI also introduces challenges to traditional legal and ethical frameworks. This conference will delve into the dual nature of AI’s impact, highlighting its applications in decision-making, workforce management, and customer engagement while addressing critical issues such as ethics, privacy, and regulatory oversight. As AI becomes a cornerstone of modern business, understanding its implications is essential for creating resilient and forward-thinking strategies.

The emergence of AI and the digital economy necessitates a rethinking of legal systems to meet these new realities. Intellectual property laws, data protection regulations, and ethical guidelines must evolve to support technological innovation while safeguarding public interests. This conference aimps to examine these intersections of law and technology, providing insights into how legal frameworks can adapt to the demands of a rapidly transforming world.

Finally, the digital and sustainable era requires businesses to reimagine their operations. To remain competitive, organizations must embrace AI-driven innovations, e-business solutions, and advanced knowledge management practices. By aligning technological advancements with environmental accountability, businesses can enhance operational efficiency, foster competitiveness, and better meet the expectations of an increasingly conscious consumer base.

By bringing together diverse perspectives, this conference aims to generate actionable insights and promote interdisciplinary collaboration. With a special focus on regional integration and the unique challenges faced by Albania, the Balkans, and the European Union, the discussions will provide a platform for addressing shared challenges and opportunities.

We invite scholars, researchers, and professionals to submit papers that delve into these transformative themes. Whether addressing theoretical advancements, empirical findings, or practical applications, your contributions will play a vital role in shaping the discourse on economics, law, and social policies in a rapidly evolving world.

We look forward to your participation and the exchange of ideas that will emerge during this conference.

Join us in shaping the future of economic and sustainable development through insightful contributions and collective expertise!

Shpëtim Çerri

Professor and Dean of the Faculty of Business and Law, University "Aleksandër Xhuvani" Elbasan
