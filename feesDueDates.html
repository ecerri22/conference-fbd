Information for Authors

The Scientific Committee will evaluate and accept the best papers based on relevance, clarity, and authenticity. Accepted papers will be published in the Conference Proceeding Book with an ISBN.

Presentation Options

Presentations can be made in person or virtually through online sessions.

Conference Languages

English / Italian

Conference Fee

35 EUR (3,500 ALL) per paper *

A participant should not author or co-author more than 2 papers.

Note

No fee for UNIEL full-time or part-time academic staff, as well as for students.

Important Dates

  • March 17th, 2025: Abstract submission deadline
  • April 2nd, 2025: Full paper submission deadline
  • April 15th, 2025: Notification of full paper acceptance
  • April 26th, 2025: Conference Day

Paper Submission

You can submit your papers at konferenca.fbd@uniel.edu.al

Venue

Faculty of Business and Law, Rruga Tomorr Domi, Elbasan, Albania
