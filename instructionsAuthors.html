Instructions For Authors

  1. Home > Instructions For Authors

Paper Guidelines

Papers must be written in English or Italian following the standard academic format.

General Formatting

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt.
  • Line Spacing: Single-spaced throughout.
  • Margins: 1-inch (2.54 cm) on all sides.
  • Paper Length: Between 5 to 10 pages.

Paper Structure

  • Title Page: Must contain:
    • Paper Title
    • Author’s Full Name
    • Institutional Affiliation
    • Email Address
  • Abstract: A summary between 200 to 400 words.
  • Main Body: Should include:
    • Introduction
    • Research Objectives
    • Methods & Data Analysis
    • Findings
    • Conclusions & Recommendations
  • References: Must follow the APA Referencing System.

Abstract Requirements

Your abstract should be clear, concise, and informative. It must include:

  • A brief introduction to the study.
  • Key research objectives.
  • Scientific methods used.
  • Key findings and conclusions.

Main Body Formatting

  • Introduction: Define the research question and objectives.
  • Methodology: Describe participants, research methods, and data analysis techniques.
  • Results: Use tables, graphs, and figures to support findings.
  • Conclusion: Summarize key outcomes and provide recommendations.

References

All references must follow the APA Referencing System.

Final Submission

Papers should be submitted in PDF or Word format following these guidelines.
